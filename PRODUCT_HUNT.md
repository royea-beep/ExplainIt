# Product Hunt Listing — ExplainIt

## Tagline (60 characters max)

Turn any website into a video tutorial in 60 seconds.

## Description (260 characters max)

ExplainIt turns any website or web app into professional explainer videos and PDF guides in under a minute. Paste a URL, get automated screenshots, narrated videos, and a downloadable guide. No editing. For devs, educators, and docs teams.

## First comment from maker (longer pitch)

Hey Product Hunt!

We built ExplainIt because we kept wasting hours turning product UIs and docs into tutorial videos and PDFs. Manual screen recording, editing, and writing steps is slow and boring.

ExplainIt automates the whole pipeline:

1. **Paste a URL** — Any public site or web app (we support login flows too).
2. **AI plans the flow** — Our agents figure out the key screens and steps.
3. **We capture, narrate, and document** — You get screenshots, short explainer videos per screen, and one clean PDF guide.

Use cases we love: onboarding videos for SaaS, internal training for tools, documentation for support teams, and quick demos for stakeholders. Everything stays consistent and up to date because you can re-run the pipeline whenever the product changes.

We support Hebrew and English (RTL-friendly), portrait and landscape, and a “Smart Mode” where you describe what you want in plain language and we generate the rest.

Try the free tier (3 pipelines/month) and see how fast you can ship a tutorial. We’d love your feedback.

— The ExplainIt team

## 5 key features (bullet points)

- **One URL → full tutorial**: Paste a URL and get automated screenshots, explainer videos, and a PDF guide in under 60 seconds.
- **Multi-step AI pipeline**: Planning, capture, video generation, and PDF creation run automatically—no manual editing.
- **Smart Mode**: Describe what you want in plain language (e.g. “ClubGG registration flow”) and we generate the full pipeline.
- **RTL & bilingual**: Hebrew and English support with proper RTL layout for global teams and educators.
- **Export-ready**: Demo page with embedded videos, downloadable PDF, and optional report JSON for integrations.

## Target audience

- **Developers** — Quick demos, onboarding flows, and internal tool documentation.
- **Educators** — Turning course materials or web apps into step-by-step video tutorials.
- **Documentation teams** — Keeping product docs and support guides in sync with the live product.
- **Product managers** — Stakeholder demos, release notes, and feature walkthroughs without editing.
